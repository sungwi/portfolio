Pokédex

Pokédex or Pokémon visual dictionary. Replicated the iconic Pokédex from the Pokémon series.
Pokémon are displayed in a card format with its index on the top page.
Each card of Pokémons is linked to its detail page as shown below.

At the bottom of the detail page, there are also links to the detail pages of the Pokémon with the previous and next Pokédex indexes.

The search bar on the top allows users to find and access the detailed pages of specific Pokémon.
