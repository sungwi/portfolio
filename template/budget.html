Budget Tracker

Group project at SFU

Given user inputs about their expenses on various items such as food, housing, or hobbies, it calculates and optimizes their spending by comparing it to the average expense statistics for undergraduates in Vancouver.

After the user selects the duration for their expenses, it provides a summary with various aspects of spending, including comments on items where expenses can be reduced.

It displays a bar graph comparing estimated costs and optimized costs, with options to select between daily, weekly, and total durations.
